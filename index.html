About

Conor Schall

Video producer and photographer based in Toledo, Ohio. I make short-form documentaries and photographs that look for the real thing — the unscripted moments, the light that almost got away, the stories people carry but rarely tell.

When I'm not behind a camera, I'm probably building something with a soldering iron, chasing trains, or cooking something that takes way too long.

Location — Toledo, OH
Focus — Documentary · Photography · Video Production
